Apply for an Export Catch Certificate

Create a UK export catch certificate to export fish and shellfish to any part of the EU or EEA or to any of these countries:

  • Iceland
  • Ivory Coast
  • Kuwait
  • Madagascar
  • Norway
  • Thailand
  • Tunisia
  • Ukraine
Find out which countries are in the EU and EEA

Start now

Before you start

You don't need an export catch certificate if you're exporting to a country that isn't in the EU or EEA and isn't listed above.

You don't need an export catch certificate if you're exporting the following:

  • farmed fish or farmed shellfish
  • freshwater fish or freshwater shellfish
  • fish fry or larvae
  • some molluscs (including mussels, cockles, oysters, and scallops)

Other ways to apply

if you need help with export catch certificates, call the MMO helpline on 0300 123 1032
